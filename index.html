Did you know that?

Only one in every 10 people know about autism spectrum disorder, while only three of them have the right knowledge.

Despite the efforts of many organizations and even states, autism awareness level is still under the desired levels, thus, society treats differently to people with autism.

The goal of this campaign is to provide people with correct information about Autism Spectrum Disorder. We need to show that individuals with autism are not so different from other people by raising awareness.

The only thing you need to do is to wear #BlueForAutism and share your picture on social media. Think colorful, Wear Blue, Show Awareness.

How Much Do You Know About Autism?

According to a research, 7 out of 10 people have not heard about autism in the World! 14% of those who stated they have heard about autism have no idea about it. And 93% who claim to have heard about it do not know the symptoms.
